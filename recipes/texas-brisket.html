Texas Brisket

Description

Smoked Texas brisket is a mouthwatering cut of beef, slow-cooked to tender perfection with a flavorful bark and a deep smoky aroma. Seasoned with a simple blend of spices, this iconic barbecue dish captures the heart of Texas with every savory bite.

Ingredients

  • Brisket
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Beef Tallow

Steps

  1. Trim the Brisket
  2. Season the Brisket using salt, pepper, garlic
  3. Smoke the brisket until it feels nice and soft and the bark has settled
  4. Wrap the brisket using beef tallow until it is poking tender
  5. Rest for a few hours, then enjoy!
