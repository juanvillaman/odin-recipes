Fish Tacos

Description

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Sour Cream
  • Mayo
  • Lime
  • Garlic Powder
  • Sriracha
  • Cotija Cheese
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Bake the Fish
  2. Combine All Taco Sauce Ingredients
  3. Toast Tortillas on Dry Skillet
  4. Assemble starting with your Tortilla
  5. Fish, followed by cheese and the taco sauce!
