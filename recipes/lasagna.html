Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made by layering pasta with meat or vegetables, cheese, and sauce, then baking it until golden and bubbly. It's easy to make, especially with store-bought sauce and pre-cooked noodles, making it a simple yet tasty meal for anyone to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
